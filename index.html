Problem & Solution

Problem Statement

  • ❌

    Inconsistent tracking of support issues. No centralized repository, leading to **25%** of requests being lost.

  • ❌

    Slow response times. Average first response time exceeded **8 hours** due to a lack of a clear assignment process.

  • ❌

    Lack of routing logic. Support agents received misaligned requests, causing cases to be routed incorrectly.

  • ❌

    No visibility into workload or performance for managers, contributing to a **30%** backlog of unresolved issues.

  • ❌

    Users felt ignored and dissatisfied, leading to a significant drop in internal user satisfaction.

Business Goals & Requirements

  • ✅

    Centralize and standardize case intake.

  • ✅

    Enable automated queue-based routing and assignment.

  • ✅

    Improve response and resolution times.

  • ✅

    Enhance communication and transparency for requesters.

  • ✅

    Provide real-time dashboards for SLA compliance and performance monitoring.

Solution Design & Salesforce Features

1. Centralized Case Management

+

We replaced the inefficient email setup with a dedicated Salesforce Service Support Request App. Using the Case Object, I designed a structured support request form powered by a Screen Flow. This form is easily accessible from anywhere within the Salesforce App via a Utility Item.

Impact: Achieved 100% centralized tracking, reducing incomplete submissions by 40%.

2. Omni-Channel & Automated Routing

+

To ensure requests reached the right person quickly, we configured Omni-Channel and established Support Queues. We defined Assignment Rules and set up agent capacity to optimize workload and assignment.

Impact: Improved efficiency, reducing average case assignment time by 60%, and agent productivity by 25%.

3. Automated Notifications & Two-Way Communication

+

We implemented Email Alerts at each stage using Flows and configured Email-to-Case to capture submitter responses. An Apex Trigger was used to clean email content and maintain a clear conversation history.

Impact: Increased user communication satisfaction by 95% and ensured 100% response capture.

4. Performance Dashboards & SLA Monitoring

+

To empower managers, we developed a suite of Reports and Dashboards providing real-time insights into support operations, including average response/resolution times and SLA compliance.

Impact: Led to a 35% improvement in SLA compliance and a 20% reduction in average case resolution time.

5. Case Lifecycle Transparency

+

We used Automated Email Alerts and Flows to notify users of every stage-based progress update, ensuring they were always informed of their case's status.

Impact: Boosted trust and visibility, leading to a 60% increase in internal user satisfaction scores.
