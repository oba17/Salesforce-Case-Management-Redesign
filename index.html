Solution Design & Salesforce Features Used

1. Centralized Case Management

We replaced the inefficient email setup with a dedicated Salesforce Service Support Request App using the Case Object. I designed a structured support request form powered by a Screen Flow, which is accessible as a Utility Item.

Impact: Achieved 100% centralized tracking, reducing incomplete submissions by 40%.

2. Omni-Channel, Queue-Based Routing and Automated Assignment

Configured Omni-Channel and established Support Queues with Assignment Rules to ensure requests reached the right agents quickly. We also set agent capacity and used a self-assignment Flow button.

Impact: Reduced average case assignment time by 60%, improved agent productivity by 25%, and eliminated misrouted cases.

3. Automated Notifications, Email Integration & Lifecycle Visibility

Implemented automated Email Alerts and a custom 'Information Request' field. Email-to-Case captures responses, and an Apex Trigger automatically extracts and cleans the email body to build a clean conversation history.

Impact: Increased user communication satisfaction by 95% and reduced clarification back-and-forth by 50%.

4. Performance Dashboards & SLA Monitoring

Developed a suite of Salesforce Reports and Dashboards providing real-time insights into support operations, including average response and resolution times, and SLA compliance.

Impact: Enabled real-time visibility for managers, leading to a 35% improvement in SLA compliance and a 20% reduction in average case resolution time.

5. Case Lifecycle Transparency for Internal Users

Automated Email Alerts notify users of every stage-based progress update (e.g., New ‚Üí Assigned ‚Üí Working ‚Üí Completed ‚Üí Closed), reinforcing transparency and trust.

Impact: Boosted trust and visibility, leading to a 60% increase in internal user satisfaction scores and reduced follow-up inquiries by 30%.
